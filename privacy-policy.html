# Privacy Policy for Veeva Vault Inspector **Last Updated:** December 2024 ## Overview Veeva Vault Inspector is a browser extension designed to help Veeva Vault administrators and developers with data export and import operations. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data. ## Data Collection and Storage ### What We Store | Data Type | Storage Location | Purpose | |-----------|------------------|---------| | Vault credentials (username, password) | Your browser's local storage | Auto-login to saved vaults | | Vault connection details (DNS, vault name) | Your browser's local storage | Quick access to your vaults | | Query history | Your browser's local storage | Access to recent queries | | Saved queries | Your browser's local storage | Reuse frequently used queries | ### What We DO NOT Do - We do **NOT** send your data to any external servers - We do **NOT** collect analytics or usage data - We do **NOT** share your data with third parties - We do **NOT** store data outside your browser ## Data Security - All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API - Passwords are encrypted before storage using obfuscation techniques - Data never leaves your device except when communicating directly with your Veeva Vault instance ## Permissions Used | Permission | Why We Need It | |------------|----------------| | `storage` | To save your vault credentials and preferences locally | | `activeTab` | To interact with the current Veeva Vault tab | | `tabs` | To open the inspector in a new tab | | `Host permissions (*.veevavault.com, etc.)` | To communicate with Veeva Vault APIs | ## Data Retention - All data remains on your device until you manually delete it - Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data - You can clear all saved data at any time through the extension ## Third-Party Services This extension connects only to: - **Veeva Vault APIs** (your vault instance) - for data operations - **Tally.so** - only when you voluntarily submit feedback (optional) ## Your Rights You have the right to: - Access all data stored by the extension (visible in Chrome DevTools) - Delete all stored data at any time - Uninstall the extension to remove all data ## Children's Privacy This extension is designed for business use and is not intended for children under 13. ## Changes to This Policy We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date. ## Contact For questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact: **Villageminds** - Website: [https://villageminds.in/](https://villageminds.in/) - Feedback: [Submit Feedback](https://tally.so/r/MeNXGE) --- By using Veeva Vault Inspector, you agree to this privacy policy.
