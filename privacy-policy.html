Privacy Policy

1 Overview

Veeva Vault Inspector is a browser extension designed to help Veeva Vault administrators and developers with data export, import, and inspection operations. This document outlines our privacy practices and terms of service.

Developer: Villageminds Private Limited — villageminds.in

We believe in 100% privacy. Your data stays on YOUR device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your information on external servers.

2 Data Collection & Storage

No External Data Collection: Veeva Vault Inspector does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data to any external servers. All operations performed by the extension happen locally on your device.

No Third-Party Sharing: Since we do not collect any data externally, we do not (and cannot) sell or share your data with third parties.

What We Store Locally

Data Type Purpose
Vault credentials Auto-login to saved vaults
Connection details (DNS, vault name) Quick access to your vaults
Query history Access to recent queries
Saved queries Reuse frequently used queries

What We DO NOT Do

  • Send your data to any external servers
  • Collect analytics or usage data
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Store data outside your browser

3 Data Security

  • All data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API
  • Passwords are encrypted before storage
  • Data only leaves your device when communicating with your Veeva Vault instance

4 Permissions Used

Permission Why We Need It
storage Save your vault credentials and preferences locally
cookies Capture active Vault session from browser cookies for seamless authentication
Host permissions Communicate with Veeva Vault APIs (*.veevavault.com, *.veevavault.cn, *.vaultdev.com, *.vaultpvm.com)

5 Data Retention

  • All data remains on your device until you manually delete it
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data
  • You can clear all saved data at any time

6 Third-Party Services

This extension connects only to:

  • Veeva Vault APIs - Your vault instance for data operations
  • Tally.so - Only when you voluntarily submit feedback

7 Your Rights

  • Access all data stored by the extension (via Chrome DevTools)
  • Delete all stored data at any time
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all data

This extension is designed for business use and is not intended for children under 13.
